What to explore

Top Sightseeings in Tartu

University of Tartu Main Building

The University of Tartu Main Building is a stunning example of neoclassical architecture, constructed in 1809. It serves as the centerpiece of the university and symbolizes Tartu’s long-standing reputation as an intellectual hub.

Tartu Town Hall Square

Tartu Town Hall Square is the vibrant heart of the city, often bustling with students, locals, and visitors. The iconic Kissing Students Fountain, a beloved symbol of Tartu’s youthful spirit, is located in the center of the square.

AHHAA Science Centre

The AHHAA Science Centre is the largest interactive science museum in the Baltic region, offering hands-on exhibits that engage visitors of all ages. It features fascinating displays on topics like space, robotics, and the human body.

Tartu Observatory

The Tartu Observatory, located on Toome Hill, is one of the oldest observatories in Northern Europe and played a significant role in scientific advancements during the 19th century.

Vanemuine Theatre

The Vanemuine Theatre is Estonia's oldest theatre, founded in 1870, and remains one of the most important cultural institutions in the country. It offers a wide variety of performances, including plays, operas, ballets, and concerts.

Tartu Cathedral

The Tartu Cathedral, located on Toome Hill, is one of the most significant landmarks in the city. Originally built in the 13th century, it is now a stunning Gothic ruin that once served as one of the largest cathedrals in the Baltics.

The Estonian Aviation Museum

The Estonian Aviation Museum, located near Tartu, showcases over 50 aircraft including military jets, helicopters, and civilian planes. Visitors can enjoy interactive exhibits like flight simulators and explore aviation artifacts. The museum also hosts an annual aviation day with exciting air shows and aerial performances.

The Widget Factory

Aparaaditehas (The Widget Factory) is a creative hub and cultural space located in the heart of Tartu. Originally a Soviet-era factory complex, it has been transformed into a vibrant creative city, housing art galleries, studios, cafes, restaurants, and more.
