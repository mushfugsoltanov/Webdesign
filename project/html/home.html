Why Tartu ?

Tere Tulemast!

Tartu is often called the intellectual and cultural capital of Estonia, home to the prestigious University of Tartu since 1632. With its rich history and vibrant academic life, the city attracts students, researchers, and entrepreneurs from around the world. Tartu’s compact size, green spaces like Toome Hill and the Botanical Gardens, and the scenic Emajõgi River make it a peaceful yet dynamic place to live. Known for its growing startup ecosystem, the city hosts major events like sTARTUp Day, fostering innovation and creativity. With an affordable cost of living, a youthful population, and a welcoming community, Tartu offers a high quality of life for residents and visitors alike.
